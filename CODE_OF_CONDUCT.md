# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, constructive feedback, responsibility, and focus on the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, hateful conduct, publishing private information, and conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Community leaders may remove or reject contributions that conflict with this Code. Report unacceptable behavior through the private contact path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
